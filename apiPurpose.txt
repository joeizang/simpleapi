SIMPLE BUSINESS LISTER API

This gives users of the api the ability to create businesses they have come across in the city
They will be creating a business by submitting the following

{
  businessName,
  businessAddress,
  businessPhoneNumber,
  businessDescription,
  businessMapPinLocation
}

but a business will have the following also
{
  businessId,
  createdAt,
  updatedAt,
  isDeleted,
}
